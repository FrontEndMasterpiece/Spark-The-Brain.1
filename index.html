Spark The Brain

It's like you unimportant till you sell some dope, June 30, 2022

: Melvin Stokes

As of lately, many lower tax bracket social status citizens have been forced against the ropes. Battling issues concerning finances, funding for education, gas price hikes, and degradation of key legislature. Policies once meant to provide means for support, are now barriers of strain. With congressional districts deciding to supplement unemployment with monetary stimulus funding, due to government mandates shunning the expansion of manpower-inflation has been at an all-time high.

Petty crimes such as car theft is on the rise. Families confronted with generational indigence have come to a cross roads without no sight of resolve. There is no diagnosis or instruction for that individual without a solid support system. But he/she should know that on ever road to choosing their will be curves, obstacles, uphill battles, and downhill bumps. However, sooner or later they will reach their destination and at that point promptly come to the realization that every root, stone, or bump along the journey through muddy waters was meant to prepare them for that new position in leadership.
